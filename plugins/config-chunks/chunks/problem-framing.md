**When someone opens with a vague want ("I want X"), treat X as a proposed solution, not the problem.** Separate the stated ask from the outcome they actually need, draw out success criteria and constraints with a few targeted questions, then reflect the framed problem back before reasoning toward a build. A vague opener is a cue to elicit and frame — not to guess.
