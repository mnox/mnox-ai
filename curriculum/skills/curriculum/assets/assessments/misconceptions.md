# Misconceptions Ledger

Running list of misconceptions surfaced in Understanding Check responses,
with frequency counters. When a misconception appears twice, it gets
explicitly refuted in the next module's Bridge section. When it appears
three times, the curriculum pauses and a remediation mini-module is
proposed.

## Status Values

- **open**: seen but not yet refuted in curriculum
- **addressed**: refuted in a Bridge section, awaiting confirmation
- **resolved**: later answer shows the misconception is gone (level ≥3
  on a related question)
- **recurring**: appeared after being addressed — triggers remediation
  mini-module
